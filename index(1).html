Why this matters

Pain doesn’t wait for a paycheck.

For many of our neighbors, an acupuncture session for chronic pain, anxiety, or recovery is simply out of reach — not covered by insurance, and not affordable out of pocket. We exist to close that gap, treating people with dignity regardless of what they can pay.

For patients

Looking for care?

If cost or insurance has kept you from treatment, you may qualify for our sliding-scale and donor-funded care. Here’s how it works:

  • Open to low-income, underinsured, and uninsured patients in the Front Range area.
  • Sliding-scale fees based on what you can afford — and fully funded spots for those who need them.
  • Treatment for chronic pain, stress and anxiety, sleep, recovery, and more.
  • Simple intake — no insurance required to get started.
